About

I build AI-driven mathematical models of tumour growth and cancer treatment response. My PhD research at the University of Birmingham develops phase-field simulations of how tumours evolve under chemotherapy and hypoxic stress — applying inverse modelling and machine learning to move cancer treatment closer to personalisation.

I have worked across Nigeria and the United Kingdom in academia, government, and research — bringing computational rigour and real-world perspective to every problem I tackle. I am also a Data Analyst at BioRTC (Wellcome Trust) and a Lecturer at QA Higher Education.
